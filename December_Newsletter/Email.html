11/3 UAA Basketball Halftime

We joined the UAA Basketball Team on the court during halftime as hundreds of 5th graders from around Anchorage watched! Two 5th graders got to compete to score the most disks with our robot, Sid.

11/4 UAA STEM Day

We participated in UAA Stem Day with our robot, Sid. Over 1600 people attended the event, many of whom stopped by our booth to try to score disks in the goal with Sid.
