VEX U: Over Under
This year UAA Robotics is competing in the VEXU 2024 competition: âOver Underâ as team ICE1. In this new game, we are tasked with moving green tetrahedron-shaped game elements into our goal to score the most amount of points. At the end of the game, we must climb 3 ft tall poles to score the most points.

So far for Over Under we created a one out of our two drive trains with 6 motors and 8 wheels. This allows us to traverse the field with incredible speed and accuracy. The second drivetrain has been CADed and is the review phase. We are currently working on prototyping the intake and tribal shooter with pneumatics and other mechanisms

On the programming side, we have completed drivetrain controls and are working on autonomous navigation with odometry and A* pathing. We are also working on sending data between the VEX Brain (i.e. computer) and a Raspberry Pi so we can take advantage of additional sensor and processor capacity.
