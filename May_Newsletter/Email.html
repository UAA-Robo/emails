4/19 RC Car Workshop
We hosted a Build a RC Car Workshop open to everyone at UAA and of our 24 slots were filled! Participants got to assemble and while their own RC car with DC motors, an ESP32, and other electronic components. Once assembled, they could create custom code or use our prewritten code to control the car with their mobile device.
