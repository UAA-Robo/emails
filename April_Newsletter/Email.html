Project Updates

VEX U: Over Under

We finished both VEX U bots over winter break, and officially named them: Scrat is the smaller bot and Scratette is the larger bot. Their names are inspired by the squirrels from the movie, Ice Age (because the current VEX U Challenge has "acorn" triballs).

First Competition. On 1/20 we competed in the Alaska Remote Skills Competition, which we hosted ourselves. The goal for the teams who competed was to score as many points as possible during a 1-minute driver controlled period and 1-minute autonomous period. We scored 62 driver-controlled points and 47 autonomous points, winning us the competition.

Although we did well for our first competition, we still learned that we have a lot to work on before our next competition (Purdue Sigbot Slam and Jam on 2/24). For one, we're redesigning our catapult on Scrat to be more reliable and shoot the triballs in a higher arc. We're adding rotational sensors to both catapults to improve reliability as well. We also learned that speed and accuracy in autonomous is crucial, so we're working on improving that with PIDs galore. Wish us luck!

CoffeeBot

CoffeeBot is kicking off in full swing this semester! If any of you are familiar with the team pre-pandemic, we used to compete in the Mars Rover Competition where we built a rover that had to complete different challenges in the dessert. We decided to bring one of the old rovers to life by re-using its end effector as our new CoffeeBot claw! Be on the lookout for more CoffeeBot updates next month!

Outreach

1/26 Girl Scouts Robotics Badge
We helped a local 4th grade Girl Scouts Troop earn their Robotics Badge by making remote control cars! The scouts learned how to use a wiring diagram, upload code to their ESP32 microcontroller, and use TinkerCAD to add designs to their car. They had a blast!

1/24 Highschool VEX Team Mentorship
West Highschool has a new VEX VRC JROTC Highschool team, the only VRC team in the state. This month, the VRC team came to UAA and we got the chance to show them our field and bots, and answer all their questions.
